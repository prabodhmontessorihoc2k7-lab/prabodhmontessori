Montessori Philosophy

The Montessori method is not just a way of teaching‚Äîit's a philosophy of life. It nurtures the child's natural curiosity through "fascinated exploration," guided by carefully prepared environments. The aim is to inspire academic excellence, nurture imagination, and awaken the human spirit.

Aims of Prabodh Montessori House of Children

  • Prepare a natural and life-supporting environment for the child.
  • Allow the child to live and learn freely in this environment.
  • Continuously adapt the space so that each child can reach their full potential ‚Äì physically, mentally, emotionally, and spiritually.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Prabodh Montessori

What age groups does Prabodh Montessori serve?

We serve children from ages 3-6 years across three programs: Pre-Nursery (M1) for ages 3-4, LKG (M2) for ages 4-5, and UKG (M3) for ages 5-6.

What makes Montessori education different from traditional preschool?

Montessori education focuses on child-led learning, hands-on exploration, and developing independence. Children learn through doing rather than memorizing, and teachers act as facilitators rather than traditional instructors.

What are the school timings?

Our timings vary by program: M1 (Pre-Nursery): 9:30 AM - 1:00 PM, M2 (LKG): 9:30 AM - 1:30 PM, M3 (UKG): 9:30 AM - 3:00 PM.

Where is Prabodh Montessori located?

We are located at 1st Cross, Ashok Nagar, Mandya, Karnataka, 571401. Our school is easily accessible and centrally located in Mandya.
